Illtide
Oct 2017 - Feb 2018
Online cooperative Action-RPG vertical slice.
In the game, three heroes, coming from 3 different villages down in the valley, must unite their powers against a common threat : The Illitide. Each of them have a unique ability that reacts to the other ones creating devastating combinations. Together they have to find the source of the Illtide to uncover its mysteries…
